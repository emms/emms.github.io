ABOUT ME

I am a creative person who has a deep desire to design consumer-friendly applications that ensure ease of access and usability. This has become an important topic, as the standards of our digital experiences have not kept pace with the sudden explosion of technology. I believe that we can develop applications that surmount this technological gap with an experience that is not only simple, but enjoyable, providing potential for enhanced use for a wide pool of consumers. My combination of business, design and technical skills gives me the ability to create more user-centric and interactive experiences that delight the people who use them.

 

PROJECTS

I made a visualisation of people killed by the police in the US utilising D3.js and TopoJSON and a data set provided by Fatal Encounters. The interactive visualisation can be explored through selecting different variables, such as age or race, to visualise by and hovering over individual counties to see data specific to them. The code can be found on my GitHub.

Somekratia is a platform for discussing public decisions made by the city of Helsinki. It is built as a single-page application with AngularJS and uses the Open Ahjo API provided by the city of Helsinki. This was a group project in which I worked on the frontend coding (excluding the map and markers) and design. The site is only in Finnish as the data is not available in English. See the code here.
