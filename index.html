ABOUT

Frontend development, to me, is the ultimate combination of my two passions: logic and creativity. Both of them, I believe, are inherently a part of coding, and both have to be included to make good, usable interface designs. The intersection of these two is where my skills get to shine.

Although my main focus is in frontend development, I am very interested in UX, user research and UI design as well. I am currently majoring in User-Centered Design in my master's studies of Information Networks. I recognize the need to constantly develop myself in this ever-changing field, and the rapid pace at which I get to face new challenges is what makes the work so enjoyable and rewarding for me.

PROJECTS

Visualisation of people killed by the police in the US

I made a visualisation of people killed by the police in the US utilising D3.js and TopoJSON and a data set provided by Fatal Encounters. The interactive visualisation can be explored through selecting different variables, such as age or race, to visualise by and hovering over individual counties to see data specific to them. The code can be found on my GitHub.

Discussion platform for decisions made by the city of Helsinki

Somekratia is a platform for discussing public decisions made by the city of Helsinki. It is built as a single-page application with AngularJS and uses the Open Ahjo API provided by the city of Helsinki. This was a group project in which I worked on the frontend coding and design. The site is only in Finnish as the data is not available in English. See the code here.
